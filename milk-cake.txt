Ingredients:
1. Thick milk
2. Sugar

Recipe:
1. Boil milk and stir continuously till it becomes 1/3rd of original quantity.
2. Add sugar and continue heating and stirring.
3. After sugar has disolved remove the milk from the stove.
4. Refrigerate it for 24hours in a vessel.
5. Remove from refrigerator and preheat gently for 2 min.
6. Invert the vessel so that the cake comes out. 
7. Cut the cake and serve.
